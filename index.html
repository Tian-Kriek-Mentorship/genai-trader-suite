❗ What Is the Liquidity‑Cycle Dashboard (v0.1)?

      
This dashboard helps traders understand where money is flowing in and out of financial markets (crypto, forex, stocks).

      
Instead of guessing when to trade, it visualizes trends and liquidity flows on two timeframes:

      
    
        
  • 📅 Daily chart – big picture
    • 
        
  • 🕐 Hourly chart – timing entries
    • 
      

      
The goal:
 Spot momentum shifts and liquidity zones so you trade with the market, not against it.
